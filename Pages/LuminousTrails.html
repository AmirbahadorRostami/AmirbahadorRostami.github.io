Amirbahador Rostami

Tech-Artist, Music Producer and XR developer

Luminous Trails acts as a gateway to a virtual world in which participants leave their Trail as they explore Toronto. their geo-locational data is translated into specific Trails visible through the augmented reality experience. The main motivating idea behind this project is the exploration of spaces around us and how each of our interactions affects these environments and how these environments impact us. A Trail is described as a geo-locational journey traveled by each user as they are moving act ross downtown Toronto, visiting different exhibitions, meeting friends, going to restaurants, or just idling.
