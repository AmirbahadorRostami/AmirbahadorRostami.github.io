BioWords is a web based Artificial Life experience, that invites the participants to write in sentences that would be interpreted into Artificial creatures that would roam the screen. Sentences written by users are processed using natural language processing algorithms to determine the Sentiment score of the given sentence. Each sentence is then broken up into individual words and each word is represents a sequence of letter which is interpreted as the DNA that creates unique a BioWord that is spawned into the virtual world. The behaviour of each BioWord is based on its own DNA and the sentiment score that it carries from their parent sentence.
